CHAPTER I

The director behind the lens

Founded by Alex Rivera in 2018, Imperial Cinematics began as a passion project to merge the grandeur of cinema with the intimacy of photography.

We believe every photograph and every frame of film should feel like a still from an epic motion picture. Our work has been featured in galleries across Europe and campaigns for global luxury brands.
